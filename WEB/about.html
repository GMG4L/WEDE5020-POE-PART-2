What we do for you?

Laundry Hub is a community based, non profit organiasation that is dedicated in helping YOU and our fellow South Africans with Laundry and making You look like THE MAN in your workplace. We notice that our fellow South Africans work endless hours in low wage paying jobs that they barely have time to do their laundry and they cant afford a washing machine that is quick and efficient making lot of our South African go to work looking dirty,poor or untidy leading them to have very low moral at their workplace. This is where we step in!. Laundry hub is fully determined in helping you with your laundry with efffiency and assurance for free. Our services are available weekly from Monday to Friday

Our Services

Our services are here to simply make your laundry life easier and cleaner. To issue our services is very easy, you either sign up with your ID and home address or book our free services online at LaundryHub.org. Once ordered, we will issue you a pickup date based on your neighborhood (Area Code). Clothes will be returned on specific dates, or you may come to collect them live. This process ensures that clothes are not lost or stolen. Our services are applicable to residents who earn lower than R5000 and are part of the working force of South Africa.

Our Services Include:

Cleaning

:

Our laundry services focus on cleaning clothes through a combination of specialized processes, equipment, and detergents designed to remove dirt, stains, odors, and bacteria, making our South Africans feel good while wearing their clothes


Iventory

Managing an inventory of clothes is a key part of our professional laundry services, considering the fact that we offer pickup and delivery or handling bulk laundry as expected

Complete Laundering & supply

Our laundry services don't just focus on cleaning clothes we also ensure the entire process is completed efficiently where applicable, clothings like linens, uniforms, or bulk orders are delivered back to clients on time and in full.

Delivery

As explained above clothings like linens, uniforms, or bulk orders are delivered back to clients on time and in full. As our delivery team are employees from the specific area we operate in. Which makes delivery fast and effiecient. The duration of clothes coming back to you, depend on the amount of clothes sent to us to be clean.
